Project Snapshot

Helping Employees Serve Customers Better

Being in customer service can be fast-paced and hectic. An interface should not be slowing anyone down.

Problem

PenFed was migrating its customer service platform to SalesForce.

This gave us the opportunity to update the design of the old system for a better customer service representative experience.

Goal

Redesign the online experience with feedback from PenFed service reps so they could better assist PenFed members.

Design Process

I conducted contextual inquiry by shadowing PenFed customer service reps as they helped members at call centers and in the branch using the existing software.

I participated in the requirements gathering to contribute to the user stories.

I collaborated with the project team to develop low-fidelity wireframes and a clickable prototype.

I used the prototype to gain feedback from the project team and customer service reps. This feedback provided insight to iterate and drive design forward.

Results

The new customer service platform was designed and delivered to the development team for production in order to help employees deliver better customer service.
