Enhancing the Credit Card Customer Experience

Our customers deserved an updated credit card application. So we gave it to them.

Problem

PenFed had not updated its online credit card application for many years.

Previous Next

Existing Credit Card Application

Goal

Redesign the online experience for members opening applying for credit cards.

Leverage the application style guide we had established for other recent application redesigns.

Increase application volume and net promoter score.

Design Process

I iterated through many flow proposals to eventually get approval from the business owner, IT, compliance, and UX.

The flow was streamlined after negotiating with compliance, reducing customer input fields, and ordering steps in a more intuitive manner.

I worked with the credit card product owner to craft email correspondence for customers receiving application updates.

We developed the content strategy and email design to highlight the most important information for the user to consume quickly.

Previous Next

Credit Card Emails

Feedback was incorporated into the application and email design before high-fidelity wireframes were designed and delivered for development.

Results

The new credit card application has received executive approval and is currently under development.
